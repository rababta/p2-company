About TechCal

What does the company do, or offer?

The company “CalTech” is a digital wall calendar producing company where customers can purchase wall calendars that can sync with their phones and laptop.

Who is the company to potential customers?

it is a company dedicated to organized individuals who want to find a way to switch from paper calendars, to a digital one that can be constantly updated based on the information they add through their phone. CalTech also aims to save the environment through reducing the use of paper and encouraging having one source of keeping up with dates, tasks and events.

Why might this company entice someone to purchase a product or service?

Individuals who like to be organized and view their day/month/year and their events/tasks on their wall/desk without having to constantly look into their phone or computer calendar apps. These calendars are also customizable based on the weather, time of the year, or just a personal customization. They also come in different sizes.
